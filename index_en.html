OCFL Workshop: OCFL Standard in Practice

Welcome to the workshop OCFL Standard in Practice. In this workshop, you will learn the basics of the Oxford Common File Layout (OCFL) and how to create, manage, and validate OCFL objects using the gocfl tool.

Workshop Content

The complete documentation and the course of the workshop are available in the ocfl_practice subdirectory:

  • Introduction & Overview – A short abstract and background information on OCFL and gocfl.
  • Table of Contents (TOC) – The step-by-step guide through the workshop.
